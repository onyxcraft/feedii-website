## Presentation Tips

### Website Walkthrough Structure

1. **Hero (10s)**
   - "This is Feedii, an AI-powered nutrition tracking app"
   - "Notice the clean design and clear value proposition"

2. **How It Works (15s)**
   - "Three simple steps: Snap, Get Data, Track Progress"
   - "All powered by AI running on the user's device"

3. **Features (20s)**
   - "Six core features, from AI recognition to privacy-first design"
   - "Note the emphasis on privacy - a key differentiator"

4. **Pricing (10s)**
   - "One-time $3.99 purchase, no subscriptions"
   - "This solves subscription fatigue in the market"

5. **Privacy/Impact (15s)**
   - "All data stays on device - unique in the market"
   - "Making nutrition tracking accessible and trustworthy"

### Handling Questions

**Q: Is this app real?**
A: "This is the landing page for our app currently in development. The AI models are being trained, and we're targeting iOS launch in Q3 2026."

**Q: How is this better than MyFitnessPal?**
A: "Three key differences: (1) AI recognition vs manual entry, (2) Privacy-first vs cloud storage, (3) One-time purchase vs subscription."

**Q: What about competitors like Noom?**
A: "Noom charges $60/month for coaching. We focus on accurate tracking at $3.99 one-time, making it accessible to everyone."

**Q: How do you make money?**
A: "One-time Pro upgrade at $3.99. With 10M+ potential users in our target market, this is a viable business at scale."
